Research & Publications

Areas of Investigation

Arabic Linguistics

Research on Arabic grammar and morphology, semantics, phonetics, and variation across regional and pedagogical contexts.

Technology & Learning

Corpus-based and computational projects for Arabic learning, including familiarity dictionaries, spoken models, and interactive learning resources.

Dialectology

Comparative studies of Arabic dialects, including regional phonological variation such as realizations of the /g/ sound in Egyptian dialects.
